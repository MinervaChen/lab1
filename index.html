Jon Snow

Jon Snow is the bastard son of the Lord, Eddard Stark. He lives a very interesting life, and has taken an oath to serve for the Knight's watch. At the point where I am watching the show, he is currently deserting the knight's watch and is living with the Wildlings on the other side of The Wall. His life is suspenseful.

This is a character from the book and show, A Game of Thrones .
